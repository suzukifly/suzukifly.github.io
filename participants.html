For Participants

Presentation Guidelines

Oral Presenters

  • Please submit your presentation file (PowerPoint or PDF) at the speaker's desk at least 30 minutes before your session.
  • Aspect ratio: 16:9 recommended.
  • Embedded fonts are strongly recommended.

Poster Presenters

  • Poster size: A0 Plus portrait (914 × 1292 mm).
  • Please set up your poster by 9:00 AM on your assigned date.
  • Core times will be announced in the final program.

Accommodation

Participants are responsible for arranging their own accommodation. Several hotels are available near Ookayama Station and Meguro Station.

Social Events

A banquet will be held on the evening of November 5 (Thu). Please register for the banquet during conference registration.
