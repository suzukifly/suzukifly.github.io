Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I attend without submitting an abstract?

A: Yes. Abstract submission is not required for attendance, but we encourage all participants to present their research.

Q: Is the conference open to international participants?

A: Yes, JDRC17 welcomes researchers from around the world. The official language of the conference is English.
