Welcome

The 17th Japan Drosophila Research Conference (JDRC17) will be held at Institute of Science Tokyo (formerly Tokyo Institute of Technology), Ookayama Campus, Tokyo. This meeting aims to bring together researchers working on Drosophila biology to exchange the latest findings and foster new collaborations.
