Welcome

The 17th Japan Drosophila Research Conference (JDRC17) will be held at Institute of Science Tokyo (formerly Tokyo Institute of Technology), Ookayama Campus.

As our premier biennial gathering, this meeting brings together researchers from diverse fields of Drosophila biology to exchange cutting-edge findings and foster global collaborations. We are especially excited to host this year's event in conjunction with the Japan-Taiwan Fly Symposium 2026.
